What We Do

ToStrip is a travel startup built by a team of five passionate university students with one shared belief: everyone deserves to benefit from tourism. Born in Cambodia, our mission goes beyond travel planning — we aim to break the cycle of poverty by empowering local communities through tourism.

We design unique travel experiences that connect travelers with authentic local culture, hidden gems, and real people behind each destination. By working directly with locals, we ensure tourism creates meaningful impact while giving travelers unforgettable memories.
